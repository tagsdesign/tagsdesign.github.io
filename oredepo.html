Ore Deposit by tagsdesign are behavior pack allow players to generate a random deposit structures, from stones, to andesite, and Iron to ancient debris.

When spawning, different dimensions such as overworld, nether and end, have different structures depending on the surface of the dimension.

Structure are made in vanilla way as possible, without any other details and design, to best fit in your survival world.

This Add-on does not use player.json, it will work properly with other add-ons.
